MORUS by davemucky & jasperslashtazkei

Morus is a vanilla+ mod for Balatro (made with Joker Forge) that adds Jokers, Tarots ect.

INSTALLATION STEPS

Download Morus
Put it into your Balatro Mods folder (located in appdata/roaming)


NOTES
Make sure your Steammodded is on the latest release, otherwise it will crash the game
